Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

The short version

Luna Suite plugins and extensions run AI entirely in your browser via WebGPU. Your content never leaves your device for AI processing. We only collect what's necessary for payment and product delivery.

What we collect

  • Payment information: processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
  • Email address: used only to send your download link and occasional product updates.
  • Purchase history: so you can redownload products you've purchased.
  • Basic analytics: anonymous page views and referrers (Cloudflare Analytics, no cookies, no personal identifiers).

What we DON'T collect

  • Your WordPress content, posts, or images processed by Luna plugins — those stay in your browser.
  • Your Notion pages, bookmarks, or any content touched by Luna Chrome extensions — local only.
  • Cross-site tracking cookies, advertising identifiers, behavioral profiling.
  • Anything else not essential to delivering your product.

Third-party services we use

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare — web hosting and content delivery. Cloudflare privacy policy.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery. Resend privacy policy.
  • GitHub — open-source code hosting. GitHub privacy policy.

AI processing

All AI features in Luna plugins and extensions run locally in your browser via WebGPU using Llama 3.2, Florence-2, and similar models. Your prompts, content, or results are never sent to Luna, OpenAI, Anthropic, or any third-party AI service. No API keys, no cloud inference, no telemetry on AI usage.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your email and purchase history at any time by emailing thecodebe@gmail.com. We'll complete the deletion within 7 days.
