FAQ

How does local AI work in WordPress?

Each plugin registers a Gutenberg sidebar panel. The first time you use it, your browser downloads a small AI model (Llama 3.2 1B for text, Florence-2 for vision) and caches it. Inference runs entirely in your browser via WebGPU — your WordPress server doesn't need GPU or any special hardware.

Does my content leave my server?

Never. All AI runs in your browser. Your posts, images, and audio stay on your device. There's no API call, no cloud service, no external dependency.

What are the hardware requirements?

A modern browser with WebGPU (Chrome 113+, Edge, Safari) and hardware GPU acceleration. Works on most laptops made since 2020. Your WordPress host doesn't need anything special — the AI is browser-side.

Lifetime and Agency automatically get every new plugin we ship, forever — voice, diffusion, and anything else. Starter and Pro include lifetime updates for plugins in their bundle, but not new releases outside it.
